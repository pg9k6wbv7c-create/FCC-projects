Bushwood Chronicle

August 9th, 1974

Webb takes down Schmells in epic battle!

Read on for more on Schmells melt-down...

By Karl

This past Sunday Ty Webb and Judge Schmells went head to head for the Bushwood Country Club Men's Open Championship. Judge Schmells held close on the front nine due to some interesting rule calls from Rules Official, Dr. Beeper.

No matter the bad luck, Ty Webb pushed through and came away victorious for the second year in a row.
